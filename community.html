the community

stakeholders

The stakeholder group consists of user representatives and interested parties that provide requirements and input to the project, or just attend to ask questions. In turn, project managers inform the group on development work, seek input on requirements, design and priority decisions, and request product testing.

The group meets weekly and is open to anyone with an interest in mdToolkit. If interested in attending a stakeholder meeting, send an email to inquiry@mdeditor.org and meeting details will be provided.

partner with us

mdToolkit project is open to collaborations. Contact the project at inquiry@mdeditor.org. Code contributions are welcome. See more.

subscribe

Subscribe to receive announcements regarding mdToolkit, such as new releases and features: mdeditor-feedback@googlegroups.com (requires a Google account). Alternatively, send an email with "Subscribe" in the subject line to: inquiry@mdeditor.org. To unsubscribe, email mdeditor-announce+unsubscribe@googlegroups.com.
